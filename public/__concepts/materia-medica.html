The detox is the protocol.

Has your busy schedule left you feeling the need to cleanse and rejuvenate your body and mind? Ancient Ayurvedic Panchakarma Detox therapy helps eliminate toxins, improve digestion, boost energy levels and strengthen the immune system.

  1. Cleanse what accumulated.

    Ayurveda believes the buildup of toxins in the tissues and blockage of channels of circulation are the root cause of many chronic conditions.

  2. Rebuild digestion and energy.

    Therapeutic massages, herbal treatments and gentle cleansing procedures working synergistically to restore balance.

  3. Go deeper, daily.

    A 7-day treatment is ideal: it is believed to sequentially cleanse and rejuvenate all 7 major bodily tissues.
