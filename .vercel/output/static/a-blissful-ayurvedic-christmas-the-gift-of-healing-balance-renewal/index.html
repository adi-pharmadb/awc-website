What Is Panchakarma Detox?

Ayurveda believes that the buildup of toxins in the tissues and blockage of channels of circulation are the root cause of many chronic conditions.

Panchakarma Detox is a therapeutic system that cleanses toxins that are already in your body and are the underlying cause of health imbalances and diseases.

Ayurveda has used these cleansing treatments to rejuvenate the mind and body since ancient times. Book a treatment and discover the difference Panchakarma can make to your wellbeing.
