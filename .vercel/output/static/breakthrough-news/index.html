What Happens During Your Medical Detox Treatment?

When you reserve your place today, here’s what happens…

At least 7 days prior to your treatment, you will attend the clinic for an initial check-up. Parking is available, so you don’t have to worry about where to put your car.

At the clinic, our fully trained Ayurvedic doctor examines your tongue and pulse to identify the seat of toxins. And then the doctor uses this information to develop a personalised treatment plan
for your health and body type.

The following week, you will return to the clinic for your tailor-made detox.

This time, our friendly staff will greet you and give you a bag of aromatic detox herbal tea to increase feelings of serenity.  You will also get a free meditation CD to aid mindfulness during your
quiet times at the clinic.

As soon as you feel comfortable, the treatments will begin, including a combination of the following…

  • Abhyanga… a gentle herbal-oil massage used to stimulate circulation, encourage the body to flush out toxins, and relax the body and mind.

  • Shirodhara… a warm herbal oil is gently poured over the forehead calming the mind, body, and spirit… as well as banishing brain fog and increasing clarity.

  • Gharsana… your skin is lightly caressed with cotton gloves & gentle scrub to cleanse clogged pores and to enhance circulation.

  • Swedana… a herb infused steam bath where your heart and head are kept cool to allow you to breathe easily. Your body is heated to aid the removal of emotional, physical and mental toxins lodged deep in the tissues.

  • Pizichill… an Ayurvedic therapist pours a continuous stream of warmed herb infused oil over the body, whilst massaging your body with special strokes. This promotes improved circulation, a cleansing of deep tissues, and a heightened state of mindfulness.

  • Udvartana… a deep herbal massage helps flush toxins from the body and restore natural vitality and zest.

Our experienced medical doctors and Ayurvedic therapists use delicately hand-crafted organic oils. We also provide delicious food, so you feel satisfied and relaxed during your time with us.
