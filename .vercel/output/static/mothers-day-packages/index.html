Mother's Day Packages 2017

3 May 2017·3 min read

This Mother’s Day, treat your mum or yourself or both! To celebrate this special day at the Ayurvedic Wellness Centre, we are offering a range of indulgent Mother’s Day packages for a limited time, all of which are valid for six months from the date of purchase:

Pure Bliss Package: Rejuvenate and Revitalise

Start with our authentic Indian head massage, which relaxes the mind and calms the nervous system helping you completely forget your ability to worry about anything. This treatment is followed with our signature treatments Abhyanga full body oleation and deeply relaxing Shirodhara, where steady stream of TWO litres of warm and soothing herbal oil is slowly and gently poured over the third eye. To indulge you even further, the next treatment is Udvartana scrub using herbal powders, then followed by the Kizhi herbal pouch massage to help alleviate aches, pains and stress. To top it all off, you enjoy a cup of our organic detox tea in our sound therapy room, as soothing Gandharva music is played in the background. This package is nothing short of pure bliss.

Benefits: Reduce fatigue and overcome stress. Feel calm, refreshed and energized.

Treatments: Champi Head Massage 15 minutes, Abhyanga Full Body Massage 30 minutes, Shirodhara 30 minutes, Udvartana Scrub 30 minutes, Kizhi 30 minutes, Music Therapy 30 minutes

2 hours 30 minutes, $450

Revitalise Package

This package begins with our best-selling full body massage, in which essential herbal oils that suit your body type help to rejuvenate and refresh your body. This is followed with Kizhi, an herb-packed pouch dipped in warm herbal oil. This works to open the pores of the skin, allowing for the release of toxins and the absorption of herbal essences. After Kizhi, spoil yourself with our Shirodhara treatment, where a stream of herbal essential oil is poured over the third eye, deeply relaxing the mind and helping to reduce stress. After this, you relax in the sound therapy room, where we serve you organic Ayurvedic detox tea as ancient Gandharva healing music is played.

Benefits: Relaxation, sound and refreshing sleep.

Treatments: Abhyanga Full Body Massage 30 minutes, Kizhi 30 minutes, Shirodhara 30 minutes, Music Therapy 30 minutes

2 hours, $375

Vitality Balance Package

Starting with a Indian head massage, which focuses on the chakra points for maximum relaxation and stress relief, this package is sure to leave you on a high note. After Abhyanga full body massage treatment, where herbal oils are massaged into the skin, you are indulged with our Udvartana herbal scrub treatment. Ayurvedic herbal scrub powder helps to tone your body and promote weight loss. The treatment works to reduce cellulite and detoxify bodily impurities. After this, our sound therapy room is where you’ll deeply connect with your inner self whilst you are served detox herbal Ayurvedic tea and relax with traditional Gandharva healing music.

Benefits: Weight reduction, body toning, vitality and deep relaxation.

Treatments: Abhyanga Full Body Massage 30 minutes, Udvartana 30 minutes, Champi Head Massage 15 minutes, Music Therapy 30 minutes.

1 hour 45 minutes, $325

Relax Package

This package begins with Champi, Indian head massage, and using special Ayurvedic herbal oils chosen to nourish the hair and the scalp. This treatment focuses on the chakra points to help relieve stress and ensure maximum relaxation. After this, your body is nurtured and indulged with our Abhyanga full body massage. This massage promotes the penetration of essential oils deep into the skin. This is followed by sound therapy, when ancient Gandharva healing music is played, as you relax and sip our authentic Ayurvedic detox herbal tea. Soothes the mind and nourishes the body.

Benefits: Relaxes and invigorates the body and mind.

Treatments: Champi Head Massage 15 minutes, Abhyanga Full Body Massage 45 minutes, Music Therapy 30 minutes

1 hour 30 minutes, $150
