Book Appointment Online

To book appointment(s) with Ayurvedic Wellness Centre online select your Appointment Type, then choose your date range. The list below will show you availability for 7 days, you can then select the suitable time slot for your appointment(s). To be able to manage all your bookings online you will need to register for a Mindbody account.

Important Booking Information

  1. To book for more than 1 person (Couples Massage) you will need to complete two separate bookings (for each person) under a different therapist. Alternatively, you can always give us a call 02 9389 2581.

  2. Please note, if it is less than 24 hours before your desired booking or if you can’t find the booking of your choice online please call us at 02 9389-2581 to book with reception.

  3. For bookings on Sundays please call reception.
