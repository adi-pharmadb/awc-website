Doctor's Consults

29 October 2015·3 min read

Ayurvedic Doctor’s Consultations in SydneyRestore your body’s natural balance with a holistic Ayurvedic assessment

At the Ayurvedic Wellness Centre in Sydney, your journey to better health begins with a personalized consultation from one of our highly qualified Ayurvedic doctors. Rooted in over 5,000 years of ancient Indian wisdom, Ayurveda views health as a harmonious balance between body, mind, and spirit—not merely the absence of disease1.

Why an Ayurvedic Consultation?

An Ayurvedic consultation offers a unique, holistic perspective tailored to you. It identifies imbalances in your physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual health and provides tools to restore equilibrium. At our centre, we believe in treating the root cause—not just the symptoms

What to Expect in Your Ayurvedic Consult

Your 60-minute consultation with our experienced Ayurvedic doctors includes:

Comprehensive Health History

Understanding your complete lifestyle, health conditions, past ailments, and emotional well-being.

Pulse Diagnosis (Nadi Pariksha)

A non-invasive technique to assess dosha imbalances, subtle body energies, and organ function2.

Tongue Analysis

Tongue examination reveals signs of toxin accumulation (ama), digestive issues, and organ health3.

Body Constitution (Prakriti) Assessment

Discover your dominant dosha(s)—Vata, Pitta, or Kapha—and how it influences your health, personality, and tendencies4.

Diagnosis of Vikruti

Identify current imbalances affecting your health so that a personalized plan can be created.

  • Tailored Wellness Plan, which may include:
    • Ayurvedic dietary recommendations
    • Herbal medicine specific to your constitution
    • Lifestyle and daily routine modifications
    • Detox or Panchakarma treatment suggestions
    • Yoga poses (asanas) aligned to your dosha
    • Breathing exercises (pranayama)
    • Meditation and mindfulness practices

FAQs

Q1: What makes an Ayurvedic consultation different from a regular GP visit?

An Ayurvedic consult focuses on holistic healing. Unlike conventional medicine that may focus on symptoms, Ayurvedic doctors assess your lifestyle, digestion, sleep, mental state, and overall energy to diagnose root imbalances and restore harmony through natural therapies.

Q2: Do I need to stop my current medication before the consult?

No. Ayurveda can work alongside conventional medicine. Please bring a list of your current medications, and our Ayurvedic doctors will assess how best to integrate Ayurvedic support without conflict.

Q3: Can Ayurveda help with chronic conditions?

Yes. Ayurveda is known for its long-term results in managing chronic issues like digestive disorders, hormonal imbalances, anxiety, fatigue, and skin conditions, among others. Your plan will include specific herbs, therapies, and lifestyle changes to support healing.

Q4: Are Ayurvedic herbs safe?

Yes, we use only high-quality, practitioner-grade Ayurvedic herbs, prescribed after carefully evaluating your constitution and current imbalance. Our herbs are safe when used correctly and under professional supervision.

Q5: What if I don’t know my Dosha?

No worries. That’s what the consult is for! Your Ayurvedic doctor will assess your Prakriti (innate dosha) and Vikruti (current imbalance) through pulse, tongue, and symptom analysis.

Q6: Do I need to do Panchakarma after my consult?

Not necessarily. Panchakarma is a deeper detox program, and your doctor will only recommend it if your condition would benefit from a full cleanse. Otherwise, lifestyle and herbal support may be sufficient.

Q7: Is the consult covered by private health insurance?

Yes, some Australian health funds offer rebates for Ayurvedic consultations. Check with your fund for details.
