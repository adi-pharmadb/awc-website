# Book Appointment Online

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/asset_header_separator.svg)

To book appointment(s) with Ayurvedic Wellness Centre online select your Appointment Type, then choose your date range. The list below will show you availability for 7 days, you can then select the suitable time slot for your appointment(s). To be able to manage all your bookings online you will need to register for a Mindbody account.

### Important Booking Information

**To book for more than 1 person** (Couples Massage) you will need to complete two separate bookings (for each person) under a different therapist. Alternatively, you can always give us a call [02 9389 2581](tel:0293892581).

Please note, if it is **less than 24 hours** before your desired booking or if you can’t find the booking of your choice online please call us at [02 9389-2581](tel:0293892581) to book with reception.

**For bookings on Sundays** please call reception.
