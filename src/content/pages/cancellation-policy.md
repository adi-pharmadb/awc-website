# Cancellation Policy

## When becoming a client of the Ayurvedic Wellness Centre, you agree to abide by our Cancellation Policy & Terms of business:

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/asset_header_separator.svg)

### Cancellation Policy

##### For all standard treatments (excluding detox treatments\*):

* Cash refunds are not given.
* Notice of cancellation must be given at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled appointment.
* ***No-shows are non-refundable — if you do not attend your appointment and fail to cancel within the required time, the full fee applies and will not be refunded.***
* If an appointment is cancelled with less than 24 hours notice, the appointment fee is still charged and may be held as store credit for a period of six months, after which the monies are forfeit. Under or less than 24 hours notice, “Minimum fee for cancellation for massage therapy only is **175.00** per person that is a our one hour fee for massage. “For 2 people or couples it is **350.00** “(one hour fee for each person).

**Due to the large amount of preparatory work and materials required for detox treatments, a separate cancellation policy applies for all detox treatments.**

##### For detox and/or rejuvenation treatments

* Cash refunds are not given.
* Bookings will only be confirmed upon receipt of a deposit amounting to 50% of the total cost of the treatment.
* This 50% deposit is non-refundable.
* The remaining balance must be paid by the end of the first session of treatment, or any time earlier.
* Notice of cancellation must be given at least 72 hours prior to the first scheduled appointment, wherein the 50% deposit becomes a store credit of the same amount which is valid for up to 6 months, after which the monies are forfeit.
* If a detox treatment is cancelled with less than 72 hours notice, a Cancellation Fee of $600.00 may be charged. In addition, the 50% deposit becomes a store credit of the same amount which is valid for up to 6 months, after which the monies are forfeit.
* You may have your treatment time shortened in case you are running late for your appointment. This will avoid any inconvenience caused to other clients on our schedule.
* We will send you a confirmation SMS 48 hours prior your appointment. This SMS will include the day and time of your appointment. Please call us 24 hours prior your appointment if you wish to reschedule. On the contrary 100% of the fee paid will be charged.
* Once detox/rejuvenation commences eg. Day 1, there is no refund due to the extensive amount of preparatory work and materials required for detox.
* In certain exceptional cases, a credit note of 50% can be given to use for rejuvenation or detox within 6 months from date of issue.
