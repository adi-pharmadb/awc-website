# Types of Skin Diseases and Ayurveda Treatment

## Acne

![Acne and Ayurveda Treatment](/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/skin-disease-300x200.jpg)

Acne is painful bumps or simply sores on the skin. The main reason for acne is a disturbance in hormonal secretion and is made worse by stress, a poor diet, lack of exercise etc.

## **Eczema:**

![Eczhema Disease and Ayurveda Treatment](/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/eczhema-300x247.jpg)

Eczema is an inflammation of the skin. It is a stress condition appearing as red, itchy, cracked and dry skin. It is one of the most common skin conditions in Australia.

## **Cold sores:**

![Cold Sores and Ayurveda Treatment](/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/cold-sore-300x232.jpg)

These are in the form of small blisters which appear around the mouth. The most common cause behind this skin issue is a viral infection (herpes virus). The reason for the activation of this virus is tiredness, fatigue, stress, alcohol, exposure to strong sunlight, cold or wind.

## Heat Rash:

![Heat Rashes and Ayurveda Treatment](/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/heat-rash-300x175.jpg)

This condition occurs when there is an excess of sweat secretion in some parts of the body such as armpits, back, under the breast, chest, groin. It appears in the form of small red spots with a lot itchy and prickling sensation.

## **Psoriasis:**

![Psoriasis Disease and Ayurveda Treatments](/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/Psoriasis-300x201.jpg)

This is associated with dry, raised red patches of skin covered by silvery scales. These patches usually appear on knees, elbow and scalp. This condition worsens with stress.
