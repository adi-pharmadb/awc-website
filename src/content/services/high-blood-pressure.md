High blood pressure or hypertension is thought to affect 1 in 3 middle aged Australians. It occurs when your blood begins pumping at a higher than normal pressure. Left untreated, high blood pressure can contribute to a number of diseases and chronic conditions including kidney failure, stroke and heart attack.

High Blood Pressure is a serious condition, but fortunately, Ayurveda can help restore the balance.

There are usually few, if any symptoms associated with hypertension though some sufferers experience headaches and dizziness. There are however a number of recognisable factors that contribute to it occurring. These include:

* Hereditary links
* The ageing process
* Obesity
* Unhealthy, sedentary lifestyle
* Poor diet, especially a diet high in processed fatty foods and salt
* High alcohol consumption
* Kidney disease

Many of these factors, particularly the lifestyle based ones, can also cause the condition to worsen.

Treating high blood pressure usually requires a multi-faceted approach which includes a lifestyle and nutritional overhaul and, depending on severity, medication.
