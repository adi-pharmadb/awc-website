## **What is Greeva Basti?Greeva Basti** is a deeply therapeutic Ayurvedic treatment that focuses on the **neck and upper spine**. The word Greeva means “neck,” and Basti refers to the “retention” of warm medicated oil within a specially created herbal dough reservoir placed over the affected area.

This gentle yet powerful therapy soothes stiffness, nourishes the muscles and nerves, and alleviates pain caused by poor posture, cervical spondylosis, tension, or stress.

At the **Ayurvedic Wellness Centre in Bondi Junction**, Greeva Basti is performed using warm, herbal-infused oils selected according to your dosha (Vata, Pitta, or Kapha) and specific health conditions to promote deep healing and balance.

### **What Makes Greeva Basti Unique**

During the treatment, a ring made from black gram dough is placed around the neck area. Warm medicated oil is slowly poured into this space and retained for a specific period, allowing the herbal properties to penetrate deeply into muscles, joints, and nerves.

As the oil cools, it is replaced with freshly warmed oil to maintain consistent therapeutic heat. This process relaxes the neck muscles, improves flexibility, and soothes inflammation in the cervical region.

A gentle massage may follow to enhance circulation and assist the detoxification process, leaving you feeling light, relaxed, and pain-free.

### **Benefits of Greeva Basti**

Regular Greeva Basti therapy provides several healing benefits:

* Relieves neck pain and stiffness
* Reduces shoulder and upper back tension
* Eases cervical spondylosis and disc compression
* Improves flexibility and range of motion
* Enhances blood and nerve circulation in the neck and spine
* Calms stress and anxiety related to chronic tension
* Nourishes muscles, ligaments, and tissues around the cervical area

### **Who Can Benefit from Greeva Basti?**

Greeva Basti is highly recommended for those who experience:

* Stiff neck or frozen shoulder
* Pain radiating to the arms or upper back
* Poor posture from long hours of desk work
* Cervical spondylosis or early signs of arthritis
* Frequent headaches or nerve compression

It’s an excellent preventive therapy for individuals who spend long hours using computers or mobile devices, helping restore neck mobility and relaxation.
