Soothe, restore and rediscover just how good your skin can feel. In Ayurvedic tradition, the key to Anti-Aging is keeping the three different doshas in balance. Anti-Aging with Ayurveda in Sydney

The Ayurvedic Wellness Centre Anti-Aging package is designed in consultation with one of our experienced Ayurvedic Consultants to perfectly suit your individual needs and desired outcomes. A unique combination of organic herbs, milk and aromatherapy essences are selected to help restore balance and vitality, infusing the body with goodness.

A number of traditional Ayurvedic rituals aid in Anti-Aging including:

* **[Abhyanga](/product/gift-voucher-abhyanga/)** or ‘herbal oil massage treatment’, which involves herbal oils being massaged into the body to penetrate deeply through the skin into the tissue and muscles.
* [**Kizhi**](/product/kizhi-gift-voucher/) or ‘herbal pouch massage’, which involves a healing pouch of herbs being applied to the body to release toxins and restore nutrients.
* [**Udvartana**](/product/udvartana-gift-voucher/), which involves a healing warm oil massage to encourage the elimination of toxins deep within the body and tissues followed by an ex-foliation treatment with an Ayurvedic herbal paste to enliven and revitalise the skin.
* [**Shirodhara**](/shirodhara-treatment/) or the ‘rebalancing treatment’, which involves a continuous flow of herbal oil being poured gently across the forehead to calm the mind and spirit.
* [**Nasya**](/nasya-nasal-head-treatment/) is an ancient Ayurvedic nasal treatment that combines a head, neck and shoulder massage with organic aromatherapy products and the application of warmed herbal oil to the nasal cavity.
* **[Pinda Swedana](/pinda-sweda/)** is a rejuvenating and deeply relaxing treatment where rice is boiled in a combination of 12 herbs and milk, then massaged into the joints and tissues.
* [**Basti**](/kati-basti/) is the Ayurvedic version of a herbal enema. This is used to draw toxins out of the colon and is generally the last stage of each daily Panchakarma regime as it releases toxins from the day’s treatments.

Each treatment is complemented by a head/foot massage.
