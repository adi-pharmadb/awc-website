At the Ayurvedic Wellness Centre, we are passionate about helping you heal stress by way of ancient, authentic treatments. Our facial massage, which features renowned OmVeda products, is certainly no exception, and is already proving to be a popular choice amongst our clients for its ability to detoxify the skin and leave you feeling revitalised.

### The Massage

In this deeply relaxing and curative treatment, your therapist cleanses, massages, scrubs and masks, leaving you feeling completely refreshed and rejuvenated. Before the treatment, we blend Ayurvedic herbs with natural, raw ingredients including honey, yoghurt, cucumber and milk to make a facial mask. This blend is then applied to the skin, helping cleanse and restore the skin, while also adding elasticity. The facial mask also balances out impurities and leaves you feeling clean and reinvigorated. You are then treated to either a hand or foot massage which releases stress by focusing on marma points (similar to pressure points). The treatment is sure to leave you feeling refreshed and nourished, turning the clock back and helping your skin to retain that youthful glow!

### Benefits

Our facial massage has a number of benefits.

* Detoxifies the face
* Aids lymphatic drainage
* Eliminates imperfections
* Revitalises the skin, helping it to retain that youthful look
* Aids blood circulation
* Removes dead skin cells
