The nose is considered the “gateway to the brain” and the root of consciousness in Ayurveda. This makes the health of the nasal passages essential for not only physical well-being but also mental clarity and emotional balance. Seasonal changes, especially from winter to spring, tend to bring about a buildup of Kapha-related symptoms

* Excess Mucus
* Congestion
* Allergies
* Sinus Issues

Nasya therapy, which involves administering herbal oils such as ‘anu tailam’ into the nasal passages, helps cleanse the sinuses and release accumulated toxins. This process is particularly beneficial as spring approaches, allowing the body to eliminate excess Kapha and any toxins stored during the winter months.

4. **Reduces Allergy Symptoms**: Spring is notorious for bringing pollen and allergens that irritate the nasal and respiratory passages. Nasya therapy strengthens the nasal mucosa and enhances its ability to filter out allergens, offering natural relief from seasonal allergies.
