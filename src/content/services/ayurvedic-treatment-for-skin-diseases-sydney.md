#### Common Skin Diseases and Ayurvedic Treatment for Skin Diseases, Sydney:

###### **Eczema**

Symptoms: Dry, itchy, inflamed skin, eczema rash.

Ayurvedic remedy for Eczema: Neem, Turmeric, and Aloe Vera are potent herbs that reduce inflammation and soothe the skin.

Diet: Incorporate cooling and hydrating foods like cucumber, leafy greens, and coconut water to pacify Pitta dosha.

Lifestyle: Ayurvedic treatment for eczema also includes practicing stress-reducing activities like yoga and meditation, as stress can exacerbate eczema.

###### **Psoriasis**

Symptoms: Red, scaly patches that may crack and bleed.

Herbal Remedies: Use Guggulu, a resin known for its detoxifying properties, and apply oils like coconut or mustard to soften the plaques.

Diet: Avoid spicy, sour, and salty foods that can aggravate Pitta and Kapha doshas. Opt for a diet rich in fresh fruits and vegetables.

Lifestyle: Engage in regular exercise and sunbathing in moderate amounts, as sunlight can help manage symptoms.

###### **Acne**

Symptoms: Pimples, blackheads, and cysts, often on the face, neck, and back.

Herbal Remedies: Neem, Tulsi, and Sandalwood can be used in face packs to cleanse and cool the skin.

Diet: Minimize the intake of oily, spicy, and processed foods. Increase consumption of whole grains, lentils, and vegetables.

Lifestyle: Maintain a regular cleansing routine, and practice Pranayama (breathing exercises) to reduce stress.
