As Vedic texts indicate, [Pitta](/what-dosha-are-you-pitta-dosha/) dosha controls metabolism along with digestion in our body.  By having excess pitta in your body, you would have increased heat, sharpness and oiliness in the digestive tract. That’s where Pitta Digest is useful.

Pitta Digest is an herbal blend that enkindles digestive fire without increasing your pitta. This cooling formula further provides an antidote to pungent foods while promoting healthy digestion.

It also:

* Maintains healthy digestive acid levels and a healthy stomach lining while cooling and calming the tissues in the GI tract
* Supports a comfortable post-meal experience
