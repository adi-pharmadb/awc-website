## **As Seen In: Wentworth Courier | Ayurvedic Wellness Centre**

*Get Peace Of Mind: Ancient Indian Practice Brings Benefits*

By Laura Trieste

As many health fads come and go, Ayurveda has stood the test of time for thousands of years. The ancient Indian practice continues to provide holistic healing, supporting those with stress, fatigue, chronic pain, insomnia, and digestive issues.

According to Rita Sagrani, the founder of the Ayurvedic Wellness Centre, “Ayurveda looks at the body holistically—physically, mentally, and emotionally.” This comprehensive system of healing addresses the root causes of imbalance in the body and mind, not just the symptoms. Ayurveda uses a combination of techniques including massage, herbal medicine, meditation, diet, and yoga to tailor a treatment that fits each individual’s unique needs.

Rita Sagrani’s journey with Ayurveda began nearly two decades ago when she sought alternative treatments to manage her condition of Crohn’s disease. “In the first seven days, I could see a radical difference,” Rita shared. “It brought my stress levels down and my mind was very calm.”

Her transformative experience led her to open the Ayurvedic Wellness Centre in Bondi Junction, a space where people can receive personalized Ayurvedic treatments. At the centre, clients can consult with a resident doctor to help pinpoint the cause of their health concerns.

One of the popular treatments offered at the centre is **Abhyanga**, an Ayurvedic massage that uses herbal oils to relax and rejuvenate the body and mind. Rita explains, “We use long strokes with a lot of oil to keep the blood circulating,” ensuring that the client’s energy is replenished, and their body is nourished.

In addition to Abhyanga, the centre also offers **Shirodhara**, an ancient therapy where warm herbal oils are poured over the forehead to induce relaxation and support the nervous system. This treatment is known for its profound ability to relieve stress and help alleviate conditions like anxiety and insomnia.

To learn more about the Shirodhara and Abhyanga treatments, or to purchase a gift voucher for an Abhyanga session, visit the Ayurvedic Wellness Centre’s website.

### FAQs: Ayurvedic Wellness Centre Treatments

#### 1. What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is an ancient holistic healing system from India that focuses on balancing the body, mind, and spirit. It combines the use of natural remedies such as herbs, diet, massage, and lifestyle changes to treat and prevent health issues. It considers each person’s unique constitution and aims to restore harmony in the body and mind.

#### 2. How can Ayurveda help with stress?

Ayurveda offers a variety of treatments to reduce stress, including meditation, herbal medicine, and therapeutic massages like **Abhyanga** and **Shirodhara**. These therapies help calm the nervous system, relax the body, and rejuvenate the mind, promoting overall well-being.

#### 3. What is the Abhyanga treatment?

Abhyanga is a traditional Ayurvedic massage that uses warm herbal oils to relax and rejuvenate the body. It involves long, rhythmic strokes that improve circulation, relieve tension, and promote relaxation. This treatment nourishes the skin, reduces stress, and helps restore energy balance in the body.

#### 4. What is Shirodhara?

Shirodhara is an Ayurvedic treatment where warm herbal oils are poured gently over the forehead (specifically on the “third eye” area) for a set period of time. This therapy calms the nervous system, reduces stress, and improves mental clarity. It is also known to help with anxiety, insomnia, and headaches.

#### 5. Who can benefit from Ayurvedic treatments?

Anyone can benefit from Ayurvedic treatments. Whether you are dealing with physical ailments like digestive issues or stress, fatigue, or anxiety, Ayurveda helps address the root cause of the imbalance. Personalized treatments are available based on your dosha (body constitution) to ensure effective results.

#### 6. Do I need to have prior knowledge of Ayurveda to book a treatment?

No, you don’t need any prior knowledge of Ayurveda to enjoy the treatments. At the Ayurvedic Wellness Centre, you will receive a consultation where the team will assess your unique needs, health concerns, and dosha. Based on this information, they will recommend the most suitable treatments for you.

#### 7. How long do the treatments take?

The length of treatments varies depending on the specific therapy. For example, **Abhyanga** massages typically last 60-90 minutes, while **Shirodhara** sessions last around 45 minutes to an hour. A full Ayurvedic wellness session may take several hours, depending on the combination of treatments chosen.

#### 8. Are the treatments suitable for everyone?

While Ayurveda is generally safe for most people, it is important to consult with a practitioner before starting any new treatments, especially if you are pregnant, nursing, or have any pre-existing medical conditions. The Ayurvedic Wellness Centre offers personalized consultations to ensure your treatments are tailored to your individual needs.

#### 9. How often should I have Ayurvedic treatments?

The frequency of treatments depends on your specific health goals and concerns. Some people opt for a one-time session to relieve stress or improve overall health, while others may choose regular treatments for ongoing support. Your Ayurvedic practitioner can recommend a treatment plan tailored to your needs.

#### 10. What should I do to prepare for an Ayurvedic treatment?

For most Ayurvedic treatments, it’s recommended that you arrive hydrated and avoid heavy meals before your appointment. For massages, wear comfortable clothing and inform your practitioner about any areas of pain, discomfort, or other concerns. After your treatment, it’s important to rest and allow your body time to process the therapy.
