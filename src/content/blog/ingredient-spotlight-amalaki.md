Amalaki promotes regularity and the elimination of toxins from the GI tract. Taken on a daily basis, amalaki works to continually remove pitta from the body, helping to reduce the chances of all types of pitta-related imbalances.

A rich natural source of antioxidants, amalaki also promotes proper digestion and strengthens immunity. Amalaki is also perfect for daily maintenance, detoxification and rejuvenation. Amalaki is traditionally used in Ayurveda to prevent the accumulation of pitta in the body and to rejuvenate the entire system.
