Triphala is one of the powerful and commonly used medicines in the [Ayurvedic](/) system of medicine. This traditional herb is prepared by combining three native fruits which can be named as [Amalaki](/ingredient-spotlight-amalaki/) (Emblicaofficinails), Bibhitaki (Terminaliabelerica) and Haritaki (Terminaliachebula).

Ayurveda mainly uses Triphala to support urinary, cardiovascular, nervous system and chronic constipation. Moreover, it also has other unique benefits such as,

* Natural antioxidant
* Supports digestion and absorption
* Revitalize the tissues
* Helps maintaining regularity

Since organic Triphala also supports weight loss, in order to achieve long term success in maintaining an optimal body weight, you can use Triphala along with a healthy diet and regular exercises. By doing so, it promotes absorption while nourishing and balancing the body.
