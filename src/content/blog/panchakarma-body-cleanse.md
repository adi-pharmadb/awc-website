## How Panchakarma body cleanse program can help improve your health

During a Panchakarma body cleanse program, Ayurveda explains that there are three prominent kinds of toxins which may be found in the body, all of which are released. The first, and most common, of these toxins is known as ama, which accumulates as a result of poor dietary and lifestyle choices. If left untreated, ama will develop into amavisha, the second kind of toxin, which arises from ama’s interactions with the sub-doshas. The third toxin, garavisha, comes as the result of our interactions with external environmental factors, such as chemicals and pollution. Ayurveda states that the accumulation of these toxins are what inevitably result in diseases over time, such as diabetes and heart disease. As such, these toxins must be dislodged as frequently as they are accumulated. It is in this manner that Ayurveda is able to target the root cause of disease.

Translating as “the five actions”, Panchakarma features a number of different [treatments](/ayurvedic-treatments/) which, when used in conjunction with one another, work to cleanse the body of toxins and help achieve “quantum healing”. Having enjoyed a relaxing holiday season, after Christmas and [New Year](/two-new-treatments-in-the-new-year/) is the optimal time to engage in a Panchakarma program. Our Ayurvedic Doctor explains Panchakarma no longer necessarily dictates five specific courses of action, but rather, is customised in a series of different steps with respect to your specific body type. It is also important to note that none of the steps in Panchakarma interfere with any medication you may be taking. The steps to your unique program may include:

* [**Doctors Consultation**](/doctors-consults/): Your Panchakarma body cleanse program begins with a detailed consultation with one of our in-house doctors, who will help you to determine the most effective means by which to eliminate toxins from your body. From here, the doctor will tailor your Panchakarma body cleanse program to meet your specific needs.
