If you’re feeling hot and bothered, add this cucumber and yoghurt salad recipe to your summer recipe list today. Cucumber’s cooling and calming nature benefits kidney functions, bladder and urinary while detoxifying the blood. Moreover, cucumber plays a major role in rejuvenating your skin and eyes due to its high water content, vitamin A, B and C.

## **Ingredients**

* 2 cucumbers
* 1/2 teaspoon of cumin powder
* 2 cups of natural yoghurt
* Pinch of salt and pepper

## **Preparation**

* Peal & grate the cucumbers.
* Add yoghurt into a bowl and whisk it until smooth
* Add all other ingredients.
* Mix well and serve.
