In Ayurveda, gut health is the starting point of all healing. When digestion is steady and clean, the body feels light, the mind feels calm and immunity stays strong. When digestion weakens, toxins begin to collect, leading to gut bloating, heaviness, inflammation in stomach, irregular bowels and long-term imbalance.

What modern wellness calls leaky gut or leaky stomach, Ayurveda understands as a weakening of the digestive fire (Agni) and accumulation of toxins (Ama) in the intestines.

**Signs Your Gut Needs Attention**

You may benefit from a detox for gut if you notice:

* Frequent gut bloating after meals
* Constipation or sluggish bowels
* Acidity or burning sensation
* Coated tongue in the morning
* Fatigue and dullness after eating
* Persistent gut inflammation

These are early signs that the body needs a digestive detox cleanse and a simple gut health diet.

Ayurveda supports gut health through steady daily habits. Begin your day with warm water for a mild stomach cleanse detox. Eat warm, freshly cooked meals at regular times, avoid overeating and late dinners, and use spices like cumin, ginger and fennel to ease gut bloating. Avoid raw and cold foods, fried or processed food, excess tea/coffee/sugar, and heavy dinners. 7–10 days of this simple routine can improve gut health and reduce inflammation in stomach.

When digestive issues are long-standing or repeatedly returning, Ayurveda recommends Panchakarma as a deeper level of care for gut health.

Panchakarma is a highly personalised cleansing process planned according to your constitution, digestive strength and level of toxin accumulation. Its purpose is to gently remove deep seated impurities from the digestive tract and tissues, allowing the body to reset its natural balance. Panchakarma supports a thorough gut cleanse detox and acts as a foundational detox for gut health. It prepares the system to respond better to diet, herbs and daily routine, making healing more sustainable. Rather than offering temporary relief, it aims to restore digestive strength at its root, helping you improve gut health in a lasting and balanced way.
