## Ayurvedic Massage Training Courses 2012

#### **Ancient Indian Therapies for Women**

*Learn to anoint, oleate, and rejuvenate with the traditional, practical Rituals of Ayurveda*

The Ayurvedic Wellness Centre now provides unique opportunities to learn Ayurvedic Massage. The study of authentic Ayurvedic rituals and practices has not been available in Australia until now.

### What You Will Learn:

#### **Abhyanga**

Seven sequences of ancient Ayurvedic Massage. The Strokes and Marma points of a full-body massage ritual with Indian Herbal oils. Relaxes and rejuvenates.

#### **Shirodhara**

Ancient treatment ritual using warm Herbal oils over the forehead for up to an hour. Brings the body and mind to a profound rest.

#### Courses include:

* An introduction to Ayurveda by Dr Shaun Matthews
* Sound Therapy for health & well-being with Mantras, Music and Sounding bowl
* Logbook for practice
* Practical Exam
* Refreshments and Lunch each day

Please Note: 90% attendance required

#### Prerequisites

* Course is designed for Women only
* Previous Certified massage qualifications are essential

#### Teacher

Jennifer Thomas RGN. BA. (Ayurvedic Lifestyle Counsellor & Massage Therapist)

These courses are guided by the teachings of Dr Ganapathi Bhat, P.S. Balachandran & Dr Ramadas from South India.

#### Required Text

Ayurvedic Massage, Introductory Course Workbook by Jennifer Thomas
