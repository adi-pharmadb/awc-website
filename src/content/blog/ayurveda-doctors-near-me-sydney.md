### **What to Expect During Your Ayurvedic Consultation**

During your initial consultation, our Ayurvedic doctors will determine both your natural doshic constitution (Prakriti) and your current state of imbalance (Vikruti). This evaluation uses the traditional Trividha Pariksha, or three-fold examination method:

1. **Inspection (Darshan):**

* Observing your physical features such as body structure, posture, skin, hair, eyes, and tongue
* Facial analysis and general demeanor to understand your innate doshic makeup

2. **Palpation (Sparshan):**

* Physical touch-based diagnosis, with emphasis on pulse reading and tongue analysis
* Pulse diagnosis helps reveal energy imbalances and underlying health concerns
* Tongue diagnosis indicates digestive health and toxin accumulation

3. **Questioning (Prashna):**

* In-depth questions regarding your symptoms, medical history, diet, sleep patterns, emotions, lifestyle, and more
* Discussion of your chief complaints, emotional wellbeing, and mental health

Once the examination is complete, your Ayurvedic doctor will create a personalized wellness protocol, which may include:

* **Dietary recommendations** suited to your dosha and health needs
* **Herbal supplements and medicinal preparations**
* **Daily routines and lifestyle modifications**
* **Yoga postures and breathing exercises (Pranayama)**
* **Meditation and mindfulness practices**
* **Therapeutic massages and treatments** such as Abhyanga, Shirodhara, or Panchakarma detox
