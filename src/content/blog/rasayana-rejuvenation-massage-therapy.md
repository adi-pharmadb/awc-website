## What is Rasayana Therapy?

Rasayana is one of the eight original branches of Ayurveda. It is the path of rejuvenation and a science of revitalisation. It is a therapy stemming from the ancient medical system of Ayurveda.

Rasayana boosts immunity, promotes youthfulness and reverses ageing. It is vital in replenishing the system on a cellular level after completing a Panchakarma Detox, or P.K. “Rasa” is a Sanskrit term used to encapsulate the purpose of [Rasayana Therapy](/rasayana-therapy/), which is to preserve, transform and replenish energy.
