## Calcaneal Spur and Ayurveda: Natural Healing for Foot Pain

Plantar fasciitis and calcaneal spur are two common foot conditions that cause pain and inflammation, making walking, exercising, and even daily activities difficult and uncomfortable. While plantar fasciitis refers to inflammation of the thick band of connective tissue running along the sole of the foot, a calcaneal spur leads to acute pain in the heel.

### Causes of Calcaneal Spur

Both conditions are typically caused by:

* Overuse or excessive strain on the foot
* Ill-fitting footwear
* Obesity
* Wearing high heels frequently
* Physiological factors such as flat feet or excessive inward foot rolling (pronation)

### Symptoms and Risks

Discomfort while walking, pain after long periods of rest, and difficulty in weight-bearing are all symptoms of either condition. If left untreated or mismanaged, these problems can worsen, increasing stress on tendons and ligaments, making timely and appropriate treatment crucial.
